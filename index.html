Rabaul History
Rabaul is better known as (Rababaul) in our Kuanua language meaning "The Mangroves". We come from a Melanesian Metrilian Society were the woman are rulers which they owns the Land and Clans. We speak the Kuanua Language and we are known as the Kuanua people of Rabaul and NOT the Tolais, Tolai in Kuanua meaning "My Friend" just a slang used in the early 1960s .
